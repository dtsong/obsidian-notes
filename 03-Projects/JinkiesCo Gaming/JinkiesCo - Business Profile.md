# JinkiesCo - Business Profile

## Overview

**JinkiesCo Gaming** is a family-owned TCG business located in North County Mall, Escondido, CA, founded by Sabrina (aka Sabby). This is a **content-first operation** with significant scale.

---

## Current Scale & Presence

| Channel | Metrics | Notes |
|---------|---------|-------|
| **YouTube** | 166k+ subscribers | Launched Jan 2025 - explosive growth |
| **Instagram** | 116k followers | @jinkiesco_ |
| **TCGplayer** | Gold Star Seller, 100% positive (6,355 ratings) | Store: "SabbyandGengar" |
| **TCGplayer Volume** | ~600 ratings/month, 6,474 completed sales | Very active seller |
| **Physical Store** | North County Mall, Escondido | Recently opened, expanding soon |
| **Shopify** | jinkiesco.com | Live Rip and Ship integration |

---

## Business Model Components

### Revenue Streams
- **Live Rip and Ship** streams (viewers purchase packs, Sabrina opens live, cards shipped to buyers)
- **Content creation** (card show coverage, "I Am Financially Irresponsible" series, etc.)
- **Physical retail** (buy/sell/trade, tournaments, supplies, collectibles)
- **Online sales** (TCGplayer marketplace + Shopify store)

### Card Show Presence
- Card Party Seattle
- LA Card Expo
- West Coast Trade Night
- Front Row San Diego Collectors Expo
- Collect-A-Con
- Local San Diego shows

## Infrastructure

### Roca Machine (At Home)
- Sorts 500-725 cards/hour autonomously
- 50+ custom sort types
- Pack Creator for bulk repacks
- Sort to Ship for order fulfillment
- Exports CSV for TCGplayer import

### Store
- Three glass display cabinets
- Multiple binders
- Sealed product shelving
- Shopify POS

## Upcoming Changes

### New Retail Space
- Larger space for more tournament seating
- More working area in back
- **Exterior-facing storefront** (outside of mall)
- Can stay open later (no 10 PM mall closing)
- **Security concern**: More vulnerable to break-ins
